Types of Graphics

Stingers
A stinger is the small custom transition between scenes or clips for videos or while livestreaming
Layouts
A custom layout would involve different graphics for your channel on Twitch or YouTube that convey information and help your viewers know who you are
Logos
A logo is a specific design for your channel that people will be able to recognize anywhere
Animations
A custom animation would apply to any animation needed for a type of notification, like a viewer donating or subscribing, to a small popup animation, for reminding your viewers to subscribe, follow, or find your socials, or even a random animation to be playing in the background
Overlays
An overlay would sit on top of your gameplay to give your channel a little more structure or organization to put some more visuals, besides your game, on the screen for viewer entertainment
Entire Channel Revamps
Revamping requires a lot of work. For a total revamp, this would consist of all of the above in some way totally putting together an entire channel design so that it all is uniform with your channel branding
Intros
An intro would consist of a small animation or graphic that plays before clips or videos
Merchendise Design
Designing merchendise would be small T-shirt designs or stickers or whatever merchendise you are planning on having available for your viewers to buy
